Get involved!


In order to construct the centre we need your support!

The budget for the Pokot Health Clinic is € 90,000, which is already completely funded by sponsors of the EPMP, such as SED NGO, the Incarnate Word Sisters and local authorities. The costs of the Pokot Resource Centre are estimated at € 81,300 of which we have raised € 25,700.

To raise the remaining € 55,600, we apply for funds and request companies for support. We also hope that you endorse our work method and the importance of this project. Every contribution brings us closer to realizing this project. Your donation will be of great significance.

To help us realize the Pokot Resource Centre, donations can be transferred to:
Bank account: IBAN: NL21 ABNA 0607 6980 04
In name of: Stichting Students 4 Sustainability
In the matter of: pokotresourcecentre

Sponsors can be given name recognition and will receive full insight in expenses and updates of the project. Thanks to the ANBI status of Students 4 Sustainability, Dutch companies can deduct their gift from their tax return. If you have any questions about the project, or if you would like to cooperate or contribute in terms of expertise or network, don't hesitate to contact us!
